About Us

 

"A platform where you can get to know of some interesting startups as well as promote yours."

Startup Promotion was founded in 2014 with the aim of supporting start-ups in India on social media. We are a completely transparent organization aimed to bring out the best out of your start-up. Our contributions are heartfelt, authentic and also emphasizes on the emotional side of starting a business. So, getting your startup noticed doesn't have to cost you an arm and leg. You just need to be smart and reach to us here at Startup Promotion. We work together towards providing global access to entrepreneurship.

 

MISSION

Encourage entrepreneurship amongst youth by providing them smooth and affordable marketing services and become a household name in the marketing sector

 

VISION

To position ourselves as a go-to resource for all start-ups and quickly become a hub for the marketing community.
